Eyepiece Case Labels

The case uses masking tape because printed labels fell off in July. Each label has a job: identify glass in darkness, prevent greasy optimism, and remind the operator that the Barlow doubles mistakes as well as magnification.

  • Ortho 7mm: planets only unless the air quits boiling.
  • Kellner 12: default for globular clusters; rubber eye cup missing.
  • Plossl 26: public-night eyepiece; forgiving, bright, always borrowed.
  • Moon filter: green rim, wrapped in lens paper from a camera shop.
