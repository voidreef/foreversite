Ferry Bell Schedule Card

Bell rope kept inside after last gull incident. Pull down, not sideways. Sideways rings count as complaint.

The office schedule is auditory because the harbor clock loses its hands in damp air. Deckhands recognize the length of a ring by how the window rattles.

05:55
first east crossing if the bell answers cleanly.
08:20
mail sack ferry; three bells after loading, never before.
12:00
market run; one bell for passengers, two for crates.
16:35
fog route decision. Long bell means speak into yellow horn.
sunset
no bell after sunset unless oars are already in the lockbox.
