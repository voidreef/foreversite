North Pier Office Drawer

Contents copied from the office drawer beneath the window where the harbor gulls line up to watch arithmetic. Dates use the pier calendar: a day starts when the bell is dry enough to ring.

This section keeps working papers rather than stories: tide tickets, freezer labels, ferry-bell rules, and the measurement board used before breakfast arguments become public.

Archivist note: pencil pressure indicates at least three clerks, one deckhand, and a child drawing boxes around the word SQUID.
