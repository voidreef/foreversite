NEGATIVE SLEEVE REGISTER — binder 2, tab C

Write only with soft pencil on the sleeve margin: roll number, date, camera, developer, and one subject clue. Ink migrates. Felt-tip is a slow vandal.

  1. Let film dry two hours after it feels dry. Dust hides in confidence.
  2. Cut 35mm into strips of six frames. Cut beside the frame line, not through the lazy end of frame 19.
  3. Emulsion faces away from the thumb when sliding into pergamine.
  4. Store contact sheet in front of matching sleeve. Circle selected frames on the sheet, never on film.
  5. Keep acetate sleeves away from damp basements; use archival polypropylene or pergamine.
