SAFELIGHT CARD — taped beside switch

Use one 7 watt red or OC amber bulb, at least 4 feet from paper, never aimed at the enlarging easel. If you can read a newspaper comfortably, it is too bright.

1. Before opening paper
Close door. Pull towel against floor gap. Turn off white ceiling switch first, then timer pilot.
2. Paper fog test
Lay a strip of paper on easel. Cover sections with coins every 2 minutes.
3. Read test
Develop strip normally. If coin edges appear after 8 minutes, move lamp farther or replace filter.

02468 minutes under safelight before development.
