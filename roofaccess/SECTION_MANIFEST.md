# Roof Access Ledger 7C — Section Manifest

Theme: roof access logs, tar paper patches, antenna farms, pigeon bands, HVAC service tags, and skyline sightlines. Early-web archival field-binder style with concrete roof-maintenance details and handmade SVG diagrams.

## Pages

- `index.html` — section landing page / roof plan with unique links to every page.
- `tar-paper-patches.html` — membrane patch register and tar paper rubbing sheet.
- `antenna-farm.html` — antenna rail inventory, masts, bearings, and cable notes.
- `pigeon-band-index.html` — pigeon band card index and observation ledger.
- `hvac-service-tags.html` — HVAC service tag string with unit readings and warnings.
- `skyline-sightlines.html` — field bearings from roof drain 3 to skyline landmarks.
