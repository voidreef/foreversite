# Copyshop Section Manifest

Theme: after-hours photocopy shop; toner ghosts, enlargement charts, lost flyers, copier error codes, rubber stamps.

## Pages

- `copyshop/index.html` — section front counter and directory.
- `copyshop/service-ticket.html` — service prices, pickup shelves, and register rules.
- `copyshop/enlargement-chart.html` — enlargement percentages and glass-bed warnings.
- `copyshop/lost-flyer-wall.html` — corkboard of copied missing/found notices.
- `copyshop/error-codes.html` — copier error display explanations and procedures.
- `copyshop/toner-ghosts.html` — maintenance sample sheet for drum/toner artifacts.
