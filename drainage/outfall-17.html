OUTFALL 17 / FLAP GATE AT LOW TIDE

Inspection window
Begin 42 minutes after posted low tide. End when mud releases bubbles in pairs.
Known trouble
Hinge pin swollen with rust. Gate opens six inches, then considers precedent.

Never stand in the oval mouth to prove clearance. The department already believes in clearance. Bring a mirror, a hook, and the old bucket with the municipal number scratched off.
