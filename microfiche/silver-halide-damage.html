Preservation rule: a bad scan can be repeated; bad handling cannot. If the film smells sharp or curls hard, stop browsing.

Useful honesty

Transcribe visible letters, bracket uncertainty, and describe damage separately. Mixing those jobs creates confident nonsense.

Reader-room folklore

The bright crescent at the edge is usually lamp reflection. It is almost never a secret moon, though nobody can stop patrons from hoping.
