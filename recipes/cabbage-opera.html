CABBAGE OPERA IN THREE BOILS

A useful recipe wearing stage makeup. Serves 4, or 2 if the matinee went badly.

Cast
  • 1 green cabbage, quartered like a dramatic letter
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, sliced thin enough to gossip
  • 1 tsp caraway
  • 1 apple, grated
  • salt, black pepper, splash of vinegar
Props
  • heavy pan
  • wooden spoon
  • lid that fits, emotionally if not physically
  • one clean towel for applause

ACT I. Melt butter. Onion enters from stage left. Cook until soft, 8 minutes, no browning unless the understudy panics.

ACT II. Add cabbage, caraway, apple, salt, pepper. Stir. Cover. Lower heat. Wait 18 minutes. Listen for the chorus of steam.

ACT III. Remove lid. Add vinegar. Cook uncovered until glossy. Serve under a lamp. Bow toward the stove.

If the cabbage squeaks, it is not done. If it sighs, plate immediately.
