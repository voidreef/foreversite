LINT COUNTY POCKET ATLAS

A small county used by foreversite pages when they need geography but refuse to be pinned to a real town. The cloth map below is clickable: river, kiln, and pigeon route each keep their own file.

County facts, copied from a tin tab: Population varies with humidity. Principal exports: lint, boiled labels, chimney soot, corrected maps. County seat: a folding chair in the map room.

The atlas cross-references old rooms without reusing their doors: barometer habits from the fog ledger, room-number handwriting from the motel directory, and radio drift from the midnight band.
