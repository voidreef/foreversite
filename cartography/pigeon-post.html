ATTICMAIL

PIGEON POSTCARD RACK

The rack belongs to the roof coop, south hinge. It records short-route messages too practical for prophecy and too damp for ordinary mail.

Card 4:
“Bring button twine.” Likely addressed to listening tomatoes, which tie themselves to secrets if neglected.
Undelivered:
A blank address with a chimney stamp. The midnight band reported wing clicks at 01:17.
Blue String route:
Chimney → bakery sign → Lint County. Last stop matches the lime kiln heat wall.
